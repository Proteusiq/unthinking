## Core Finding

The missing paper paradox: if a paper exists in paper_list.md but not in the filesystem, does it support the thesis?

## Key Evidence

- 100% of missing papers cannot refute the thesis
- 0% of missing papers can support the thesis
- The gap itself is evidence that even systematic literature reviews are subject to human error
